As you can see, I have this player pool tracker. There is an issue now where some players completed games are not pulling through (for example, players from Michigan, Duke, Gonzaga, and Nebraska) Their totals are not pulling through from the round of 32 even though they were pulling through earlier. Can you please investigate, patch the error and upload the new index to github.

Summary

  • Investigated the Round of 32 regression and traced it to the admin-side sanitize routine in index.html: it was trimming completedPts down to gamesPlayed, but gamesPlayed represents wins, not total completed rounds. That meant eliminated teams could lose the score from their final loss round during sync/reconciliation. I patched that logic to retain gamesPlayed + 1 completed rounds for eliminated teams while keeping the stale-data cleanup behavior for active teams.

  • Confirmed the affected examples in state.json already carry the expected Round of 32 values, including Michigan, Duke, Gonzaga, and Nebraska players, so the patch now prevents those values from being stripped back out on admin load/sync.

  • Committed the fix on the current branch as f3e8e3b with message Fix completed round retention for eliminated teams, and created the PR metadata as requested.
